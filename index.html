About Mrs. Tomei

Certified Feline Master Groomer

As a Certified Feline Master Groomer (CFMG), I specialize exclusively in cats. This certification represents the highest level of achievement in feline grooming, ensuring your cat receives expert care from someone who truly understands their unique needs.

I offer both mobile house call services and in-studio appointments, giving you the flexibility to choose what works best for you and your cat. Many cats feel more comfortable being groomed in their own home, which is why I bring my full grooming setup right to your door.
